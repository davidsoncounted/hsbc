Eligibility

To be eligible for a Premier Credit Card:
  • You must hold an HSBC Premier Account
  • You must be a permanent UK resident or hold a valid UK visa*
  • You must be 18 years or older

If you have an HSBC UK credit card, you're eligible to apply for another if you haven't applied for an HSBC credit card within the past 6 months, and the card isn't the one you currently have.

 

Please note, if you currently hold a Classic, Student, or Premier Credit Card with us, you will not be eligible for this product.
