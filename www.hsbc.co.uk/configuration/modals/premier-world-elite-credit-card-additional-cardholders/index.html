Additional cardholders

For an annual fee of £60, adding your partner, a family member or friend (18 years or over) to your account is a simple way to get more from your HSBC Premier World Elite MasterCard.

 

The £60 annual fee for any additional cardholder will be refunded for Jade customers.

 

Key benefits

 

  • additional cardholder will be able to access airport lounges
  • additional card earns 2 points for every £1 of eligible spend in sterling currency and earn 4 points for every £1 of eligible spend in non-sterling - points are added to main cardholder's balance

Sterling currency includes transactions made in the UK, Jersey, Guernsey and Isle of Man, as well as transactions overseas where you choose to pay in sterling rather than local currency.
